How Britney Spears and Lisa Rinna Maintain Such Radiant Skin

Skincare secrets of the stars! Celebs like Britney Spears and Lisa Rinna are sharing the secret to their radiant glow — they’re just using Rodan + Fields!

Spears has long been a fan of the brand, ever since her mom, Lynne Spears, introduced her to it.

“I’m obsessed because of my mom, who works with Rodan + Fields face-care line,” the Piece of Me performer has said, noting the brand’s Macro-Exfoliator, Reverse regimen and Anti-Age Redefine Regimens among her favorites.

While the glam singer is known for her fierce hair and makeup looks while performing, offstage she’s a bit more low-key.

“I am more of a skincare girl,” she’s admitted, choosing those products over others for hair or makeup.
