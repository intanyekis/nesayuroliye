Freddie Highmore | Wife, His Secret Marriage, &amp; Girlfriends

For many years, Freddie Highmore wife has been the focus of media speculation. While Freddie Highmore has been in the public eye since he was a child, he has always sought to keep his personal life private. When it comes to divulging his behind-the-scenes existence, he is undoubtedly a man of few words. In reality, many people still regard him as a single man.

But a few years ago, he surprised his admirers by announcing that he’d married quietly. However, the English actor has never provided any additional information since then. There are also a few photographs of him and his wife together.

So, just who is Mrs. Highmore? Read that, as well as other details such as how the couple met, how their wedding went, when they actually married, and so on.

The First Time We’ve Heard of Freddie Highmore’s Wife

During his appearance on Jimmy Kimmel Live in September 2021, Highmore disclosed the news of his marriage and wife for the first time. After host Jimmy Kimmel pointed out the new ring that the British actor was sporting, Freddie reacted to the topic of his marital status.

The Finding Neverland star said that he married his wife that summer. In terms of the big news, Highmore stated,

It is, indeed, a wedding ring. Yes, I married.

The Vault actor went on to say,

It’s amusing. People have been asking me whether I’m married since I’ve been wearing this ring, so I thought I’d explain.

While discussing the news, Highmore cast shade, saying that he wasn’t going to jump up and down on the couch on the talk program to convey his delight.

The remark was a jab at Mission Impossible star Tom Cruise’s notorious jumping on the couch scene during his 2005 interview with Oprah Winfrey. Cruise reacted in this manner while discussing his own marriage to his then-wife, Katie Holmes.

He explained that he is as happy as a Brit can be and that he is married to a “very wonderful woman now, so yes, I feel very happy.” Highmore also disclosed that his wife, like him, is British. The actor went on to say that he was having difficulty accepting words and vocab like “married man,” which seemed extremely ancient to him.

Freddie, Bertie’s brother, said he and his wife hadn’t actually used it yet, but when people asked, they just showed their rings.

The former child star hasn’t revealed much about his marital life. Even during his appearance with Jimmy Kimmel, the actor refused to reveal any wedding details. Obviously, the wedding photos have yet to be released.

Other Females Freddie Highmore dated before he married.
