Vanessa Hudgens Talks about Threesome in Spring Breakers

“Spring Breakers” is one of the most controversial movies in recent times. It is full of sexual scenes. There are some other hot issues too. In fact, some people are even saying that it could be among the most immoral films ever. Vanessa Hudgens was an important part of the movie. Vanessa says that she and a few of the other actors were feeling some anxiety before the filming, it however fell into place once they started.

Vanessa says that the threesome scene with James Franco and Ashley Benson was particularly bad. It made her nervous. She was very conscious about her appearance in the movie. Vanessa reveals that she was terrified of appearing nude in front of the camera. Ashley Benson was of course very cool about it, the three managed to pull it off very well in the end. The threesome scene in “Spring Breakers” was shot very aesthetically, it did not look vulgar at all.

Harmony Korine, the director, is happy with how it turned out. He is proud of the team but the one person whose performance surprised everybody was Selena Gomez. She is otherwise known to have a good girl image. Nobody expected her to work in such a movie.

Selena has done a stellar job in Spring Breakers. She and Vanessa are very good friends they always hung out together during the filming. Vanessa was apparently asked to lose weight for the film, she visited McDonalds with Selena one last time before starting her weight-loss regimen.

Spring Breakers is running successful. It features James Franco in a rather unusual role. The threesome scene in particular is among the boldest scenes, Franco did a beautiful job with the two ladies.
