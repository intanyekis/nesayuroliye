Matt Jackson Facts

  • Jackson and his brother spent 18 years of their life in Rancho Cucamonga before relocating to Hesperia, California in 2003.
  • He was billed from Rancho Cucamonga, California.
  • Matt is a self-taught wrestler who began wrestling in his backyard wrestling ring alongside his brothers Nick and Malachi.
  • Under The Young Bucks stage duo, Matt rose to fame for his work in New Japan Pro-Wrestling (NJPW) and Ring of Honor (ROH).
  • Matt is childhood friends with fellow wrestler Dustin Cutler.
  • He made his professional wrestling debut at the Alternative Wrestling Show/C4/Revolution Pro event on August 8, 2004.
  • Matt started his independent wrestling company High Risk Wrestling (HRW) in October 2004.
  • He is a huge fan of wrestling journalist Dave Meltzer. Matt and his brother Nick named their finisher after Dave calling it the “Meltzer Driver.”
