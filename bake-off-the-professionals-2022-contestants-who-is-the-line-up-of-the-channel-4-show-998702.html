Bake Off The Professionals 2022 Contestants: Who Is The Line Up Of The Channel 4 Show?

Bake Off The Professionals

Teams of professional pastry chefs compete in Bake Off: The Professionals, a British television baking competition, in two different challenges. It is a spin-off of The Great British Bake Off, and on March 29, 2016, BBC Two broadcast the debut episode. Tom Kerridge hosted the eight-episode first season of the show, and Benoit Blin, Cherish Finden, and Claire Clark served as judges. Angus Deayton hosted the second season, but Claire Clark did not serve as a judge. After the BBC decided not to continue the third season, it was relocated to Channel 4 and joined The Great British Bake Off. Bake Off: The Professionals is the new title of the Channel 4 television programme, which is hosted by comedians.

Bake Off The Professionals 2022 Contestants Heat 1

Boglarka & Krisztian

Boglarka and Kristian, a Hungarian couple, are in charge of the cuisine at the brand-new Jó Szerencsét restaurant in Gloucestershire. They care for their two young children at home while they are not working in the kitchen. Emanuele & Mariola

Emanuele and Mariola, closest friends from the private member club Eight Club London, are members. Even though they have only been coworkers in the Eight Club kitchen for a short while, they have forged a strong relationship. Emanuele works as a head pastry chef but also enjoys concerts and music, while Mariola is a skilled tattoo artist.

Jeffery & Venus

After starting his patisserie career in Hong Kong's five-star kitchens, Jeffery eventually moved with us and created his own company, A Little Sweet, where he collaborates with his sister-in-law Venus. Despite Jeffery's previous success as the World Chocolate Masters champion, the duo is trying to win over the Bake Off: Professionals judges with their creations.

Nathan & Kevin

Nathan, the team captain, has a 20-year career. Prior to arriving to London, he worked in country clubs and restaurants in Paris. He also worked at Harrods, Rothschild, and The Sanderson. His buddy Kevin, age 26, comes from Nantes, France, and is now pursuing a master's degree. Kevin has previously had positions at The Dorchester, Harrods, and Shangri-La.

Robert & Mireia

Since 18 months ago, Robert and Mireia have both been employed at the storied Levens Kitchen. Before relocating to Cumbria and working at The Castle Dairy, Robert started his career working for Gordon Ramsay at Claridges, Le Caprice, and Windows. While this was going on, Mireia began cooking in Barcelona, where she was recognised internationally for her patisserie abilities.

David & Cydrick

The restaurant Maison Mayci, which David and his brother opened in Birmingham, is being represented by French chefs David and Cydrick. Having met while attending culinary school and working at Michelin-starred restaurants, the two have been friends for 35 years.. These are the contestants that have lined up in heat 1. Enrico & Antoine

Prior to moving to the UK to work at Bibendum at Claude Bosi and Sushi Samba, Head Pastry Chef Enrico received his training in some of Italy's most prestigious restaurants. Antoine, meantime, has worked as a chef at renowned London eateries like Claridge's after relocating from France to the UK four years ago.

Jemima and Zack

Puddles Bespoke Patisseries, a London-based business that specialises in high-end cakes, afternoon tea, and desserts, is represented by Jemima and Zack. After relocating from Devon to the big city and having previously worked at The Fat Duck, Dominique Ansel, and Clause Bosi, Jemima established the company three years ago. Zack, a fellow pastry chef, was born in Trinidad and has experience working in France and the USA. He's been friends with Jemima since they first met five years ago at The Fat Duck.
