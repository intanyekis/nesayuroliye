Netflix Rumored To Be Planning 'Rebelde' Season 2 And 3 After First Reactions

After six years off screen, Rebelde has returned to the halls of this iconic school with a fresh group of students eager to make their mark on the Music Excellence Program.

Fans were left already “needing season 2” and confirming “the nostalgia was definitely there.”

Rebelde is a Mexican teen drama series that premiered on Netflix on Jan. 5. Although the Netflix series is considered a reboot, it is actually a continuation of the iconic 2004 Mexican telenovela of the same name.

Rebelde Is A Reboot Of An Iconic Mexican Telenovela

Although the characters in the new streaming series are new, it is set in the same universe.

It follows a new generation of students attending the prestigious Elite Way School as they try their best to win the Battle of the Bands. Every teen character has their own struggles as they try to navigate growing up, finding love and friendship and stardom.

It doesn’t help that Elite Way School have a threatening rival called the Lodge, trying to stop them from reaching their full potential. Fans have loved this update on the iconic Mexican telenovela. Many have Tweeted their adoration for the attractive cast of characters, rooting for their relationships and copying their stylish looks.

The cast of wannabe stars includes Azul Guaita, Sergio Mayer Mori and Franco Masini. One returning cast member includes Estefanía Villarreal, who plays Director Celina Ferrer in Netflix’s Rebelde. Celina, appeared in the original series of Rebelde and now appears as the director.

What do we know about the second season of Netflix’s Rebelde?

Advertised as a miniseries, it appears that season 2 and season 3 may be in pipeline. Given the conclusion of the first season, many fans are begging for a conclusion. After those end credits, which make a huge revelation fans are begging to see where it leads the characters.

Netflix may be contemplating a three-season run for the revival of Rebelde, as the original series did. But it’s still up in the air until Netflix gives it the green light.

So, when can we expect to learn more regarding the renewal of Rebelde’s second season? Generally, Netflix waits until after the first season has aired to assess the popularity and viewing figures before waiting to renew a show for further seasons.
