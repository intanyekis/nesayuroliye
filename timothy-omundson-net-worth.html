Timothy Michael Omundson (born July 29, 1969) is an American actor. He is perhaps most notable for his supporting roles as Sean Potter on the CBS television series Judging Amy, Eli on the syndicated series Xena: Warrior Princess, and as Carlton Lassiter in the USA Network series Psych. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
