Who is Torrey Craig?

Torrey Craig is an American Professional basketball player.

Torrey Craig plays for the Brisbane Bullets of the Australian National Basketball League(NBL).

He was named an honorable mention All-American and Atlantic Sun Conference Player of the Year in 2012.

In 2017, he became NBL’s Best Defensive Player.

Torrey Craig: Professional Life and Career

Craig is a professional NBA player. His professional career first started in 2014 in Carnis Taipans in 2016.

He also played for Wellington Saints in 2015-2016, Brisbane Bullets 2016-present, and Gold Coast Rollers 2017-present.

Torrey has been awarded NBL Best Defensive Player (2017), All-NBL Second Team (2017), NZNBL champion in 2016, NZNBL MVP(2015), NZNBL Most Oustanding Guard (2015), 2 times NZNBL All-Star Five (2015,2016), AP Honorable mention All-American (2012), 3 times First-team All-Atlantic Sun(2012-2014), Atlantic Sun Freshman of the Year(2011).

Craig was named the SCBCA 1A player of the Year after averaging 24 points, 13 rebounds, 3.5 assists, 2 steals, and 2 blocks per game.
