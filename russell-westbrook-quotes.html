Top 25 Russell Westbrook Quotes

It’s easy to recognize Russell Westbrook as he is an American professional basketball player. He played for the Houston Rockets of the National Basketball Association (NBA). Talking about his awards and achievements, he is a nine-time NBA All-Star and gained the NBA Most Valuable Player Award.

After playing college basketball for the UCLA Bruins, Russell Westbrook earned third-team all-conference honors in the Pac-10. He became very successful, earning great honors by playing even in college, and now he has been regarded as one of the most prominent NBA Stars. Besides basketball, he is also a great fashion designer.

Now, focus on the top 25 Russell Westbrook that are listed below.
