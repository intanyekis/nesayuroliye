Srikanth Meka Career

Srikanth enrolled at the Madhu Film and TV Institute of Acting in Hyderabad in 1990 and finished a one-year acting training. People's Encounter, his debut film, was released in 1991. Early in his career, Srikanth had modest roles as a villain and supporting actor. With the film One by Two, he made his breakthrough as a leading man. He has played the lead in over 100 Telugu films. Taj Mahal, his first big film as a lead actor, was released in 1995.
