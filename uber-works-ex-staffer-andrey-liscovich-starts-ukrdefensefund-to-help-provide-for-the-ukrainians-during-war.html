Uber Works Ex Staffer Andrey Liscovich Starts Ukrdefensefund To Help Provide For The Ukrainians Duri

Andrey Liscovich, a former Uber executive is making headlines after he left San Francisco for Ukraine to run a volunteer logistics operation with his former Uber colleagues to help Ukraine’s volunteer fighters.

Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky captivated the hearts and minds of many people throughout the world, who are rushing to assist the country in its horrific war with Russia.

Andrey was encouraged by Zelenskiy’s, Kyiv Mayor Vitali Klitschko’s, and other politicians’ promises to stay and oppose the Russian invasion, as were many other former Ukrainians.

So, on February 26, Liscovich flew from San Francisco to Rzeszow, Poland, boarded a train to Przemysl, Poland, hitched a ride with firefighters to the border, and walked into his homeland on foot.

Andrey Liscovich: Uber Works Ex Staffer Starts Ukrdefensefund To Help The War

Andrey Liscovich, an Uber Works ex-staffer, a few other Ukrainian volunteers, and seven former Uber colleagues in the US have developed an operation that transports boots, phones, portable chargers, cables, tourniquets, and protective gear to pick up places for volunteer fighters.

In his birthplace of Zaporizhzhia, where the Russian military just captured a nuclear plant, the former CEO of the now-defunct Uber Works staffing firm is now the unofficial procurement manager for the Ukrainian Territorial Defense Forces.

Initially, his organization raised $40,000 from friends, relatives, and former Uber coworkers. They’ve already spent two-thirds of it, and they’ve set up a website to coordinate supplies and monetary donations.

Andrey Liscovich Wikipedia Explored

Liscovich was born and raised in the southeastern Ukrainian city of Zaporizhzhia, then earned a Ph.D. at Harvard University before working for Uber Technologies Inc. from 2017 to 2020.

He departed San Francisco for Ukraine just over two weeks ago, but he spent the majority of February visiting friends in Russia. The 37-year-old entrepreneur got two armed personal guards and a new job directing procurement for the volunteer forces in his hometown.

Andrey was working on a new venture in San Francisco, his home for the past seven years, just two weeks ago.

Andrey Liscovich Net Worth Explored

The net worth of Andrey Liscovich is estimated to be around $5 million. He was a former Uber executive at Uber Works who worked for more than 3 years.

Andrey was a former director of technology at RA Capital Management. He also worked as a vice president of data science at Shuddle.

He’s exploring his professional networks in Asia, Africa, Europe, and the United States for tactical gear, shoes, mobile phones, first-aid kits, power banks, and even socks and underwear, which he’ll ship back to Ukraine for distribution to volunteer fighters.
