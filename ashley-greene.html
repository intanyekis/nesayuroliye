Ashley Greene News - Us Weekly

Name:Ashley Greene

Hometown:Jacksonville, FL

Birthday:February 21, 1987

About

Ashley Greene had her first big break playing Alice Cullen in the 2008 film adaptation of the mega-successful Twilight books by novelist Stephenie Meyer.Greene moved to L.A. right out of high school to pursue an acting career, and broke into Hollywood with roles in Fox’s MADtv and Ashton Kutcher‘s Punk’d.The actress finished filming the Twilight sequel New Moon, out in November 2009, and is signed on for its sequel Eclipse. Greene can also be seen alongside Twilight costar Kellan Lutz in their new movie Warrior, currently in post-production.Greene has been linked to Gossip Girl‘s Chace Crawford and Joe Jonas.
