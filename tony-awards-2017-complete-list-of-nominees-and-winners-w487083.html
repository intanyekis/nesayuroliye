Tony Awards 2017: Complete List of Nominees and Winners

Broadway’s finest rejoiced at Radio City Music Hall in New York City on Sunday, June 11, for the 71st Annual Tony Awards. Kevin Spacey hosted the star-studded event.

The ceremony included performances by Leslie Odom Jr. (Hamilton) and Cynthia Erivo (The Color Purple), last year’s leading actor and actress winners, respectively. The Radio City Rockets also graced the stage.
