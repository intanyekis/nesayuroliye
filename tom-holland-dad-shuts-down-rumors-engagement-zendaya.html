Tom Holland's Dad Shuts Down Rumors That He's Planning To Propose To Zendaya

Highlights

  • Tom Holland and Zendaya have a loving relationship that fans adore, but there are no marriage plans for now.
  • Despite rumors and speculations, Tom Holland's dad clarified that there will be no wedding in the Holland household yet.
  • Both Tom and Zendaya are happy living together and are focused on their relationship without any pressure to get married.

There's no question that Tom Holland and Zendaya are the ultimate power couple right now. Watching their relationship grow from a screen romance in the Spider-Man movies to a very loving, healthy partnership has been a pleasure for fans, and practically everyone in the world is rooting for them.

The Truth About Tom Holland And Zendaya's Relationship

Fans loved the idea of Tom Holland and Zendaya together even when they denied they were a couple.

For a while now, there have been rumors that the couple was headed towards marriage after deciding to move in together, but Tom Holland's father recently spoke up, asking fans to pump the brakes. Apparently, while they are very happy together, there are no plans for marriage in the near future.

Tom Holland's Dad Responds To Rumors That He And Zendaya Are Getting Married

Dominic Holland, Tom Holland's dad, has decided to weigh in on the rumors about the Spider-Man power couple. Tom and Zendaya have been together for several years now, and fans can't get enough of them. Their relationship has been a fan favorite ever since it was revealed back in 2021, and every time they are spotted they look incredibly happy together and completely in love.

Because of this, and recent reports from sources, rumors of an impending engagement have been going on. However, according to Dominic Holland, fans should temper their expectations. When asked about his son's relationship with Zendaya, the actor's father spoke candidly.

"They are a happy couple, so who knows? But there are no marriages in the Holland household yet," he stated.

Despite the fact that they are apparently not planning their wedding right now, the father seemed to leave the door open, so it might still be something the actors want.

Dominic also opened up about how the Holland family deals with Tom Holland's staggering success and explained that they make sure to keep him a down-to-earth person.

"Tom has three brothers and a mum and dad who don't treat him any differently. We all want our kids to do well. But I always try to keep him grounded," he assured.

Tom Holland And Zendaya Have Reportedly Discussed Marriage

Despite Tom Holland's father's clarification, it's clear Tom Holland and Zendaya are very committed to each other. The couple recently moved in together and has been very happy in this new stage of their relationship.

A source said a little over a month ago that Zendaya has "no doubt [that] Tom is her person," and moving in together was "the best decision they’ve ever made."

"They’re just happy to be together," the insider went on. "There's no pressure to get married at all. Moving in together just seemed like the natural next step to take in their relationship. It feels right."
