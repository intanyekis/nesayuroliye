How Did Harrison Ford Meet Calista Flockhart? Timeline of Their Relationships

Harrison Ford got married twice before he finally met a woman that completed him. Her name is Calista Flockhart, and they had an extended courtship period before they eventually got married — here's everything we know about their relationship.

WHO IS HARRISON FORD’S WIFE?

The first time Harrison Ford ran into Calista was at the 2002 Golden Globes, and they hit it off quickly. The actor had been 60 when he fell in love with Calista, and in 2009, he spoke about their first meeting.

He revealed that his biggest surprise was that he was still capable of having a long-term commitment with another person.

Their first date was in 2002, but it was also attended by James Marsden, who was there at Calista's behest. They had a nice dinner and returned to Ford's home to have drinks and listen to music until the actor signaled Marsden to make himself scarce.

Harrison Ford and Calista Flockhart on November 18, 2018 in Hollywood, California | Source: Getty Images

The couple was completely taken with each other, and by 2009, they were engaged. Then, in 2010, they decided to spend the rest of their lives together and got married in New Mexico, where the talented actor had been filming “Cowboys and Aliens.”

The ceremony was held at the governor’s estate in Santa Fe and was performed by Governor Bill Richards and was officiated by New Mexico’s Chief Justice, Charles W. Daniels.

After the wedding, Harrison became stepfather to Calista’s son, whom she adopted at birth, while she became stepmom to the four kids he had from previous relationships. Their marriage is bravely standing the test of time, and the pair’s love is still blowing hot.

HOW MANY CHILDREN DOES HARRISON FORD HAVE?

Throughout his years of acting, the talented Harrison Ford became a father four times. His first kid was named Ben Ford, and the second was called Willard Ford, while the third and fourth were named Malcolm Ford and Georgia Ford, respectively.

They are all products of his first two marriages which represented his initial attempts at love before he finally settled.

Harrison Ford and Mary Marquardt in June 1977 in New York City | Source: Getty Images

He tied the knot for the first time in 1964 with Mary Marquardt, and together, they welcomed his first and second kids: Ben and Willard.

Ben was born in 1967, while his brother arrived two years later in 1969, a decade before Harrison and his wife decided to go their separate ways.

Harrison Ford and Melissa Mathison on April 7, 1983 in Beverly Hills, California | Source: Getty Images

The actor got married again in 1983 to Hollywood Screenwriter Melissa Mathison with whom he had a son named Malcolm in 1987 and a daughter called Georgia in 1990.

They ended the union in 2004, and Melissa passed on ten years later following her battle with cancer. After the marriage ended, Harrison stayed away from the altar for years until he met Calista at that event.
