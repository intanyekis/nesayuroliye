A Look Back at Jamie Chungs Magical Wedding Her Stunning Bridal Dress and Ring

The holidays are a time for celebrating and for some, it is a perfect time to host a wedding. 

Take Jamie Chung and Bryan Greenberg for example. The couple tied the knot in 2015 at the El Capitan Canyon Resort just outside of Santa Barbara over Halloween weekend, and it was a beautiful affair to say the least.

The nuptials were part of a three-day event that began with a “boos and booze” costume dinner party and Mexican buffet and costume contest in honor of the Halloween weekend. 

In an interview with Martha Stewart Weddings, Chung shared the reason behind the couple’s decision to have Halloween/Wedding party. 

We thought it would be a fun way to break the ice, and it was a blast seeing how creative everyone got. 

Jamie Chung’s Wedding Dress and Ring

On Halloween, the couple exchanged the vows they’d written themselves in a scenic ceremony surrounded by the California mountains. Chung looked stunning in a silk and tulle Monique Lhuillier wedding dress. 

The beautiful gown boasted sheer sleeves and ice-like embroidery, and the cut-to-the-knee slit and tulle going on in the back added a dash of allure to her look. 

To keep the actual wedding dress a surprise, Chung swapped her gown for a long-sleeved, short lace dress by Jonathan Simkhai during the scheduled family photos and wedding party portraits. She then switched back to the Monique Lhuillier number for the main event. 

The actress had the perfect bridal looks on her special day, but equally stunning was her wedding ring. The three-piece Tacori set had a large round diamond ring set on a split-shank pavé band, which she wore with a vintage scalloped diamond band. 

The actress even uploaded a close-up photo of the rings after getting too many questions about it. 

Jamie Chung and Bryan Greenberg Are Happily Married

The three-piece set is frequently featured in her Instagram uploads. She doesn’t always wear all three pieces together, often opting for just one band at a time. 

In a recent photo she shared on her husband’s birthday, Chung penned a heartfelt wish in the captions and uploaded a photo of the couple holding hands and on Chung’s hand was the beautiful ring. 

The couple frequently gives the world a peek into their married lives through their social media, much to the deight of fans who have been shipping them since their dating days. Although they had crossed paths years ago, Chung and the One Tree Hill alum didn’t start dating until early 2012.

In December 2013, Greenberg proposed to Chung during a visit to her California hometown, and in October 2015, they tied the knot. Even amid their busy schedules, the couple still finds time for each other and appears to be going strong till today.
