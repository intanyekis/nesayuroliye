Margot Robbie, Ryan Gosling And The Stars Of Barbie, Ranked By Net Worth

Undoubtedly the hottest 2023 cinema ticket in town, Greta Gerwig's Barbie movie is supposed to be an unexpected, possibly feminist take on the story of the world's most famous doll.

While details of the plot are being kept under wraps (though it is believed to be in the vein of The Truman Show), what we do know is that the cast of Gerwig's third directorial feature is to die for.

Barbie and Ken are played respectively by Margot Robbie and Ryan Gosling, with the cast rounded out by Will Ferrell, America Ferrera, Kate McKinnon, and Michael Cera, to name but a few. Let's take a look at these celebrities' net worth, which are set to be considerably increased by their Barbie salary and back-end points.

12 Barbie's Emma Mackey Has A Net Worth Of $150,000

Emma Mackey, known to Sex Education fans as the rebellious Maeve, is part of the cast of the Barbie movie. The casting announcement came after fans campaigned for years to have her in the same movie as Margot Robbie, given the two stars' resemblance.

As of last year, Mackey had a net worth of $150,000, mostly due to her work on Sex Education (via The Tab). However, this has likely increased since, given the actress' work on Disney's Death on the Nile and Emily, a biopic on the life of author Emily Brontë.

11 Doctor Who And Barbie's Ncuti Gatwa Has Earned $1 Million In His Career

Nucti Gatwa, aka the first Black actor to play the main role of Doctor Who in BBC's beloved sci-fi show, has been cast in Gerwig's movie as another Ken.

Related: Ncuti Gatwa Talks About The Importance Of Playing A Black Queer Teen On Sex Education

The Sex Education star has a net worth of $1 Million, but this is likely to go up with his stint as the Time Lord.

10 Hari Nef Has A Net Worth Of $1 Million

You and And Just Like That... star Hari Nef will next be seen in the Barbie movie as another version of Barbie.

The actress has a net worth of $1 million from her modeling career as well as her writing and acting gigs.

9 Bridgerton And Barbie's Nicola Coughlan Is Worth $1.8 Million

Bridgerton's very own Penelope Featherington aka Nicola Coughlan is also part of the cast of Barbie, co-written by director Gerwig and her partner, filmmaker Noah Baumbach.

The Irish star, also appeared in acclaimed comedy series Derry Girls, has a net worth of $1.8 million (via Mirror).

8 Marvel And Barbie Star Simu Liu Has A Net Worth Of $4 Million

Shang-Chi's Simu Liu is part of Barbie's huge cast, and will portray the role of another Ken.

The Canadian actor has currently a net worth of $4 million from his work on comedy Kim's Convenience and his Marvel debut in Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings (via Celebrity Net Worth).

7 Insecure And Barbie's Issa Rae Has $8 Million In The Bank

Insecure creator Issa Rae will portray the role of another Barbie in Gerwig's movie.

The actress, writer, producer and comedian has a net worth of $8 million from her multiple gigs, which include her starring roles on Netflix's The Lovebirds as well as creating HBO Max shows Sweet Life: Los Angeles Rap Sh!t.

6 SNL Star Kate McKinnon Has A Fortune Of $9 Million

Kate McKinnon has earned quite some money from her ten-year-long stint on Saturday Night Live. The comedian has also appeared in a number of movies, including the all-female reboot of Ghostbusters alongside her SNL pal Leslie Jones, Kristen Wiig and Melissa McCarthy.

Related: Why Did Amy Schumer Pull Out Of The Barbie Movie?

McKinnon was cast in the Barbie movie in an undisclosed role which will likely increase her current net worth of $9 million.

5 Barbie's America Ferrera Is Worth $16 Million

America Ferrera was among the first stars to be cast in the Barbie movie, though we do not know much about her role at present.

The Ugly Betty actress has more recently appeared in NBC comedy Superstore. She is also a director, having been behind the camera for Netflix's prematurely axed series Gentefied, where she also served as executive producer.

She currently has a net worth of $16 million, according to Celebrity New Worth.

4 Michael Cera Has A Net Worth Of $20 Million

The star of Scott Pilgrim Vs. The World, Michael Cera will bring his awkward appeal to the Barbie movie in a yet-to-be-disclosed role.

The Juno star has a net worth of $20 million, earned from his film and theater acting roles as well as his career as a musician and producer.

3 Barbie Protagonist Margot Robbie Has Earned $26 Million So Far

The absolute star of the Barbie show, Margot Robbie plays the titular heroine, for which she received a salary of $12.5 million.

Related: Did Barbie Make Margot Robbie The Highest-Paid Actress In Hollywood?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, the Birds of Prey actress is worth $26 million. Robbie doesn't earn her income just from her acting roles, but she also has her own production company, LuckyChap Entertainment, behind projects such as Oscar-winning movie Promising Young Woman and Netflix's Maid.

2 Barbie's Ken Ryan Gosling Has A Net Worth Of $70 Million

The Drive protagonist portrays Barbie's life partner Ken in Gerwig's romantic comedy. Earlier this year, Gosling was spotted roller skating with Robbie on set, the two stars in characters as the Mattel couple, rocking matching neon outfits.

The Canadian actor has many successful roles under his belt, having proved his romantic chops in The Notebook, Crazy, Stupid Love, and La La Land. He has a net worth of $70 million.

1 Will Ferrell Has Earned A Whopping $160 Million In His Career

The actor, comedian and producer is said to play the role of Mattel CEO in Barbie — and we have a feeling he's going to be the villain in this one.

Ferrell has been in the entertainment business for a long time, with his multiple gigs earning him a whopping $160 million as of 2022.

Barbie will be released in cinemas on July 21, 2023.
