DJ Akademiks and GloRilla Go At It After She Blocks Kai Cenat

GloRilla recently blocked Twitch streamer Kai Cenat on Instagram, with many believing it was over his negative review her song,”Cha Cha Cha.” DJ Akademiks got caught in the crossfire, and he and the Memphis rapper ended up going back and forth over social media.

“God damn, motherf**kin’ Akademiks,” she said via Instagram Live on Sunday (Oct. 29). “Ni**a, your name ain’t no motherf**kin’ A-K. Ni**a, you ain’t never shot no A-K in your motherf**king life, ni**a. I’m talking about Akademiks, ‘Free KC.’ And that’s all I gotta say. F**k y’all.” Evidently, the “Tomorrow 2” rapper thought that the Twitch streamer was actually in jail when in reality he is feigning being locked up as part of his seven-day livestream.

The former Everyday Struggle host responded on X, formerly known as Twitter, and had a lot to say about the CMG rapper. “Glorilla is a F**king Idiot,” Ak said. “She really think Kaicenat is locked up. Her label must have humbled her stupid a** in realizing… Bih.. u aint bigger than the program.. and Kaicenat is valued way more than u. She tried to cop a plea to him but tried to diss me in the process. LMAO”

DJ Akademiks didn’t stop there. “Glorilla is officially a GOOFY,” he wrote in another tweet. “She said her and Kaicenat beef is some street sh*t lol.. This CHick is literally delusional…. Yo Gotti. deactivate her twitter. @GloTheofficial ur a f**king moron. LMAO. u have street beef with KAICENAT?”

The polarizing media personality even made assumptions about GloRilla’s intent in all of this. “@GloTheofficial JUST TO MAKE SURE U SEE THIS u GOOFY. IF U TRYNA SUCK KAI D**K. DONT DISS ME u GOOFY A** H*E. JUST APOLOGIZE TO THE NI**A. WAT I GOT TO DO WIT IT.”

This was all sparked by her recent single “Cha Cha Cha,” which came out at the end of September. The track features Fivio Foreign and is part of CMG’s latest collaborative effort Gangsta Art 2: Reloaded. Check out Gangsta Art 2: Reloaded below.
