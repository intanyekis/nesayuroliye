Career

Philip Martin Brown made his television debut in the UK as Sam Potter in the film A Horseman Riding By (1978). From 2006 to 2013, he participated in 160 episodes of the BBC One school drama series Waterloo Road, spanning nine series. He has also acted in a number of British dramas, including Heartbeat, Midsomer Murders, Hetty Wainthropp Investigates, New Tricks, and Doctors, where he played Seth Mercer for ten episodes. Prior to Waterloo Road, his most notable drama role was Eddie Vincent on Casualty.
