Anson Snowdeal Missing Ellsworth, Maine State Police In Search Of Missing Person

0SHARESShareTweet

Anson Snowdeal Missing – In a concerning turn of events, the Maine State Police Troopers from Troop J are urgently seeking the public’s assistance in locating 41-year-old Anson Snowdeal. Snowdeal was last seen in Ellsworth early yesterday morning when he left an apartment on Water Street on foot, notably without his phone or wallet. His sudden disappearance has prompted authorities to launch a search and confirm his well-being.

Anson Snowdeal is a 41-year-old resident of Sullivan, Maine, whose whereabouts are currently unknown. At the time of his disappearance, Snowdeal was dressed in tan work pants, a gray t-shirt, a gray hooded sweatshirt, and a black jacket. This detailed description serves as crucial information for individuals who may have seen him or come into contact with him.

Authorities believe that Anson Snowdeal may be in the Surry or Blue Hill area, both located in Hancock County. While he resides in Sullivan, the search has expanded to neighboring regions as part of the effort to locate him and ensure his well-being. Public cooperation in these areas is vital to the success of the search and the safety of Mr. Snowdeal.

The Maine State Police are urging anyone who has information about Anson Snowdeal’s current location or has seen him since his disappearance to come forward. Community members and residents in the Surry, Blue Hill, and Sullivan areas are particularly encouraged to be vigilant and report any relevant information.

If you have any information about Anson Snowdeal’s whereabouts, please contact the Maine State Police at 207-973-3700, extension 9. Your cooperation is invaluable in assisting law enforcement in their efforts to locate Mr. Snowdeal and confirm his well-being.
