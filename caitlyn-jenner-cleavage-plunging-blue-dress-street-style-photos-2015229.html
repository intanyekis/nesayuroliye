Caitlyn Jenner Works Her Cleavage in Plunging Blue Dress: Style Photos

Working her assets! Stretching the season just a little bit longer, Caitlyn Jenner stepped out in Malibu on Monday, Sept. 21, rocking an adorable summer dress with a plunging neckline.

After a trip to Starbucks (girl's gotta fuel!), Jenner, 65, hit the streets in a blue dress with a cleavage-flaunting neckline, gathered right below the bust. She accessorized with gold necklaces, a chocolate briefcase-style purse, and molten cross-strap wedge sandals.

On the beauty front, the I Am Cait star, who has become an LGBT advocate and icon since announcing her transition earlier this year, modeled her go-to hairstyle—loose curls—with a pink lip and a crimson manicure.
