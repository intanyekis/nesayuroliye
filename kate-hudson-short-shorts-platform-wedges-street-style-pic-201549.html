Kate Hudson Wears Short Shorts, Platform Wedges: Street Style Pic

Holding on to summer just a little longer! Kate Hudson stepped out of her NYC hotel on Friday, Sept. 4, toasting the holiday weekend in the Big Apple in leg-flaunting denim short shorts. (Have we mentioned #Goals?)

PHOTOS: See who made Us Weekly's Best Dressed List!

Clutching a cup of coffee and a newspaper, the Mother's Day actress, 36, who just topped the Us Weekly 2015 Best Dressed List, modeled a black and white tank top and Daisy Dukes. She carried a rainbow-hued Etro purse, and finished the ensemble with height-boosting Salvatore Ferragamo platform wedges.

On the beauty front, Hudson, who has two sons, Ryder Robinson, 11, and Bingham Bellamy, 4, rocked minimal makeup and straight shoulder-length hair.
