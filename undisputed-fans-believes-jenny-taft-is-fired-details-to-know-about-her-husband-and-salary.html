Undisputed: Fans Believes Jenny Taft Is Fired Details To Know About Her Husband And Salary

Is Jenny Taft fired from the show Undisputed as many fans believe she did? Find out what changes it could bring to her personal life, salary, net worth, and husband.

Jenny Taft is an American personality who hosts the television sports show Skip and Shannon: Undisputed.

The show is presented on the Fox Sports 1 channel. Taft is also known for her husband, Matt Gilroy, an American ice hockey player.

Taft was involved in a verbal feud live on TV almost over a week ago with Skip Bayless. Many fans were offended for criticizing a senior host. Many also believed that she got fired for being unethical.

Is Jenny Taft Fired As Fans Believe?

Fortunately, no, she was not fired. Unfortunately, this means she still gets to keep earning the chunky salary she gets.

The feud revolved around the weight gain of the Cowboys coach Mike McCarthy. Bayless questioned his ability to motivate his athletes when he himself wasn’t up to date with his physical fitness.

Taft replied, saying if Bayless was criticizing McCarthy’s physical appearance and not a fair shot. Bayless came back saying it was a fair shot.

They both went back and forth until the main host, Shannon, interrupted them, convincing Bayless that they were on the same team. Then, the show got cut into a commercial break.

So far, there are not any comments from the show’s executives. However, many believed Taft was fired due to her not appearing on the show. Nevertheless, she is also a reporter and could be busy elsewhere.

Jenny Taft Husband

Besides being a host, Jenny Taft is also famous for her famous husband, Matt Gilroy.

Gilroy currently plays in SC Rapperswil-Jona Lakers, a Swiss National League team. He is a defender. Previously, he played for clubs like Florida panter, New York Rangers, Tampa Bay Lightning.

Taft married her husband in 2015. The husband and wife dated for a few years before taking their vows. So far, the couple does not have children.

Like her husband, Taft’s father, John Taft, was also an ice hockey player for Detroit Red Wings. However, his career did not last very long. John also went to the 1976 Olympics.

Jenny Taft Salary And Net Worth

Jenny Taft receives a salary of $33K-$88K, who also estimated her net worth, $2 million.

As she is not fired from the popular sports show, the salary will continue to flow in her bank account. On the other hand, her husband, Matt earned a salary of $4.8 million, says Hockey One Zone Plus.

Combining the husband and wife’s net worth, they have a net worth of $6 million.
